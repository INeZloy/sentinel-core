6. Verdict

The German Ambient Assisted Living and remote patient monitoring market is growing rapidly rather than saturated, driven by demographic aging, shortages of caregivers, and strong government support for digital health. However, the attractive market comes with exceptionally high barriers to entry. A student-built product is unlikely to succeed initially as a fully certified medical device competing with Abbott or Philips. A more viable strategy would be to position Sentinel Core first as a caregiver-oriented safety and wellness platform (e.g., activity, location sharing, non-diagnostic vital-sign tracking) and validate user demand before investing in medical certification. If clinical features and reimbursement are long-term goals, they should be approached in partnership with healthcare providers or established medical-device companies rather than attempted from day one.
